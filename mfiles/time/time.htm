TIME

Matlab m-files for various time functions.

Maurice A. Tivey 2006

dectim- Convert decimal hours to hhmmss.

   hhmmss=dectim(time);

hmstim - Convert time in hours, minutes, secs to decimal hours

    time=hmstim(hour,minute,second);

ijuldy - Calculate day and month from input Julian day and year.

   [month,day]=ijuldy(jday,year);

juldy - Calculate Julian day from input day and month and year.

   jd=juldy(iday,mon,year);

sec_from_midnight - Compute seconds from midnight.

   y = sec_from_midnight(hours,minutes,seconds);

sec_to_ymdhms - Compute year month day hour minute second from seconds since Jan 1, 1970

    [year,month,day,hour,minute,second] = sec_to_ymdhms(secsfrom1970);

sec2hms - convert seconds in day to hours, minutes, seconds

   [hours,minutes,seconds]=sec2hms(seconds);

timdec - Convert hhmmss (Alvin) time format to decimal hours e.g. 133030 => 13.5083

   dtime=timdec(hhmmss);

timhms - Convert time in decimal hours to hrs mins secs

   [hour,minute,second]=timhms(time);

ts1970 - Calculates time since 1970 in seconds given year and decimal julian day 

   seconds=ts1970(year,julianday);

ymdhms_to_sec - This function calculates the number of seconds from Jan-01-1970 to the time specified by the args 

   secsfrom1970=ymdhms_to_sec(year,month,day,hour,minute,second);

zulu - Convert decimal time in hours to GMT by entering timeshift in hours 

   out=zulu(time,shift);
