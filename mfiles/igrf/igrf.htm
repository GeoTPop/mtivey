magfd - Compute the magnetic field at a specific location on Earth given the latitude and longitude and the date using the appropriate International Geomagnetic Reference Field (IGRF) model

    XYZT=magfd(decimal_year,1,0,COLAT,ELONG);
 
where COLAT is the colatitude (ie 90-latitude) in decimal degrees and 
ELONG is decimal degrees longitude from -180 to +180
XYZT is an array of four values [X,Y,Z,T] where 
X is the north component, 
Y is the east component     
Z is the vertical component +ve down, and 
T is total field.
